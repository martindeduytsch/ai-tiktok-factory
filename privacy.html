Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

AI TikTok Factory is a private creator automation tool used by the app owner to generate and publish short-form videos to their own TikTok account.

1. Information Collected

The app may access basic TikTok account information through TikTok Login Kit, such as the authenticated user's basic profile information, only to confirm that the correct creator account is connected.

2. TikTok Tokens

After the user authorizes the app, TikTok access and refresh tokens may be stored locally on the user's machine. These tokens are used only to maintain authentication and publish videos through the TikTok Content Posting API.

3. Video Publishing

The app may upload and publish videos generated locally by the user. The app does not publish to TikTok unless the user has authenticated the TikTok account and enabled the publishing workflow.

4. Data Sharing

The app does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Data is used only for authentication, video generation, and publishing to the authenticated TikTok account.

5. Local Storage

Generated videos, captions, logs, configuration files, and authentication tokens may be stored locally on the user's computer. The app owner is responsible for protecting these files and keeping authentication tokens private.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as TikTok APIs, text-to-speech services, news APIs, or stock media APIs depending on the user's configuration. Each third-party service may have its own privacy policy and terms.

7. Data Retention

Locally generated files and tokens remain on the user's machine until they are manually deleted by the user.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app owner through the GitHub repository associated with this project.
